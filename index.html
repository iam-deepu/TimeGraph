How to Use TimeGraph

📅

Schedules

Time blocks on your timeline — lectures, study sessions, breaks. No status; they represent your timetable.

✅

Tasks

Actionable items. Can be independent or linked to a schedule (e.g., "Complete Ch.1" under "Self Study").

🔁

Custom Recurrence

Set schedules to repeat on specific days like Mon/Wed/Fri.

🔔

Custom Reminders

Use presets or enter any custom minute value for reminders.

👆

Long-press

Long-press a task for options: Edit, Reschedule, or Delete.

↕️

Drag & Drop

Long-press and drag schedule cards on the timeline to move them.
